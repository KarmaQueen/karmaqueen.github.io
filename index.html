Welcometo Our ISOM3320 Project

DESCRIPTION: Our Java game was inspired by Valve's masterpiece, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. Our game is an action-packed top-down shooter with a variety of unique guns along with many exciting and dynamic item pickups. The enemies are also clever in hunting you down. Be careful.

INSTRUCTIONS: The hero's movement is controlled with the WASD keys. To aim, use the mouse and to fire, simply left-click. R to reload, and M to mute the music. Gain points through pickups, killing enemies, and by surviving. However simple as these instructions may sound, proceed with caution.
